The MOD Story 📖

It all began in a small suburb of Pretoria, South Africa. Beertjie, a tiny white Toy Pomeranian with the biggest heart, noticed his owner struggling with missed deliveries and unreliable couriers. Being the problem-solver he is, Beertjie decided to take matters into his own paws.

In 2020, MOD (Midgets Out for Delivery) was officially established. What started as Beertjie delivering neighborhood parcels quickly exploded into a worldwide sensation. His dedication, fluffiness, and uncanny ability to navigate through traffic became legendary.

Today, MOD operates in 195 countries, has delivered over 50,000 packages, and maintains a perfect 5-paw rating. Beertjie has become an international celebrity in the logistics world — though he still prefers belly rubs over awards.
